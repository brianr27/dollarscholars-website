What's covered in the class?

Earning
We cover various ways to earn income, including through interest and work. Kids learn how to identify their own unique skills and talents that can help them earn income. They also learn that their level of education correlates with the types of jobs they can obtain and the amount of income they can earn. Students present their own ideas of how they can earn money and work as a group on ways to improve their plans.
Saving
In our classes on saving, we discuss the importance of saving and teach kids how to save, including how to set short-term and long-term goals. Kids enjoy choosing something to save for and then working out how to achieve their goals with the help of their fellow students.



Spending
In our classes on spending, kids learn about various ways to pay for things and the concept of debt. Additionally, the students learn how to critically examine advertisements before spending their money.

Entrepreneurship
We focus on how to start a business. This lesson is one of the students' favorites, as they enjoy presenting their own business plans and hearing feedback from their fellow classmates. The class inspires the students to be creative and collaborative.
Budgeting
We discuss the importance of budgeting and how to budget your money. The kids then participate in fun activities to practice budgeting their money.
Giving
We discuss the importance of giving and learn about various charities and ways to give.
